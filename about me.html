Mission Statement

A little sweetness in every bite.

I would like to start off by saying thank you for visiting my website.
My name is McCall Manyor, I am currently finishing up my Senior year at the University of Tennessee in Chattanooga.

When everything got shut down in 2020 due to the global pandemic, I started baking to pass time. During this time I was able to prefect my craft while learning how to make more items. I shared many items that are on this website with family and friends and they enjoyed every spoonful. They are the ones who inspired me to start this business.

The plan for the future of this business is to grow slightly bigger but not too big to where it is my main focus. I would love to keep it as a side job that still brings in a great profit but doesnt feel like I am actually working.
